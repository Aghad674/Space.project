Types of Stars

Star Type Image Description
Red Giants Large, cooler stars that are in the later stages of their lifecycle.
White Dwarfs Small, dense stars that are the remnants of medium-sized stars.
Supernova A powerful explosion of a star, marking the end of its life cycle.
Neutron Stars Extremely dense stars, formed from the remnants of a supernova.
